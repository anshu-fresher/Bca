ABOUTS BCA

Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA). It is a three-year undergraduate degree program in computer science, which is offered by various colleges in Dhanbad under Binod Bihari Mahto Koyalanchal University, Dhanbad (BBMKU). The BCA program typically covers a wide range of topics related to computer science, including programming languages, data structures, algorithms, computer systems, databases, and software engineering. Students who complete the BCA program in Bermo are prepared for careers in software development, computer programming, data analysis, and other related fields. They may also choose to pursue further studies in computer science or related disciplines at the graduate level
